we give back!

10% of all proceeds will go to

Bhai Kanhaiya Charitable Fund, Inc.

This charity, established in 1994, was chosen because of its well-rounded, representative-of-Sikh-values mission: To feed the hungry, educate the poor, to treat the sick and ailing and to bring the message of Almighty God to one and all. The trust provides for a girls' college, school, and hospital. It also supports a free monthly magazine named ‘Sewa Jyoti’ to help convey the message of Guru Granth Sahib to 14,000 homes in India, the USA, the UK and Canada. With its current focus on the Cancer Pandemic ravaging the state of Punjab, as of now, funds will be going towards the expansion of the hospital.
